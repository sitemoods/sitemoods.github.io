Digital Signage

Captivate your customers by displaying products and promotions on digital screens, improve the visibility of your menu, display the ready order number, direct customers to the free cashier, or take the opportunity to train your team.

Our technology and our team automate and operate the screens of your store chain, to display your content in a way intelligente.

Applications:

Front Play:

A franchise needs to standardize its communication and at the same time regionalize parts of its content. With Moods service, this is possible. Organize what your screens display, by store groups and timelines. Eliminate costs with outdated methods and drive sales with more dynamic communication for greater ROI.

Menu Board:

Products, and promos becomes much more attractive on digital screens. Make your fast food stand out in the food court. Take advantage of the same screen to display the ready order number.

Queue on TV:

Direct the customer to the free cashier, a natural evolution of the old led numbers, take the screen advantage to display your brand's content. Also, uur system collects queues information to generate statistical reports that support management in making strategic decisions.

Enterprise TV System:

Broadcast your brand's culture to those who live it every day. Training content, work safety tips, news that boost your team's morale. With Moods service, you can have a powerful, low-cost, high-efficiency enterprise communication network.
